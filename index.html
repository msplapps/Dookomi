About App

Dookomi is a fun, cool and easy way to buy and sell things around you.

Easy setup

Install, Sign up and post Ads free of cost on the go. Posting Ads on Dookomi is very easy and works just like the Ads in the local newspaper.

Snap & Post

Now post anything unused in 30 secs. Take a picture, Set a price and that’s it. Yes, that’s it. Once posted, you can easily manage your Ads within the app on the go.

Near you

We realise that buying and selling is easy when it happens nearby. Our App shows you cool items that are up for sale at great prices around you. Hop across to pick them up from your neighbourhood.
